Early Careers

Start Your Consulting Career With AmbiDer

Our internship tracks are designed to give students real engagement exposure, not shadowing. Every intern is staffed on active client work.

Programs

Three Tracks, One Standard of Rigor

Campus Hiring

Full-time placement track for final-year students, leading directly to an Analyst offer upon successful evaluation.

  • Duration: Final semester onboarding
  • Eligibility: Final-year students, all disciplines
  • Benefits: Pre-placement offer track, mentor assigned
Apply for Campus Hiring

Summer Internship

An eight-to-ten week immersive program for penultimate-year students, staffed on live client engagements.

  • Duration: 8–10 weeks (May–July)
  • Eligibility: Penultimate-year students
  • Benefits: Stipend, housing support, PPI eligibility
Apply for Summer Internship

Winter Internship

A shorter, focused four-week program ideal for students seeking early exposure to consulting case work.

  • Duration: 4 weeks (December–January)
  • Eligibility: Second and third-year students
  • Benefits: Stipend, certificate, LOR on completion
Apply for Winter Internship
What You'll Gain

More Than an Internship

Every intern is treated as a working member of the engagement team, with the guidance to make that responsibility manageable.

Dedicated Mentor

Paired with a senior consultant for weekly guidance and feedback.

Client Exposure

Direct participation in client workshops and data-gathering sessions.

Structured Training

Consulting toolkit workshops covering frameworks, modeling, and delivery.

Return Offer Track

Top-rated Summer and Campus interns receive pre-placement offers.

Internship FAQ

Quick Answers

Eligibility varies by track — see each program card above for the specific academic year requirement. All disciplines are welcome to apply.

Yes, both Summer and Winter Internships include a monthly stipend. Campus Hiring transitions directly into a full-time offer with a full Analyst salary.

Select Apply for the relevant track above, complete the online application, and upload your resume. Our campus team will follow up on next steps.
