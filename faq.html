Careers FAQ

Answers to the questions we hear most from candidates.

Browse Current Job Openings on the Careers page, open the role you're interested in, and click Apply Now. You'll be asked for your details, a resume, and a short cover letter.

Yes, you're welcome to apply to more than one open role if your background is genuinely relevant to each. Please submit a separate application for every position, since each is reviewed by the relevant practice team.

Eligibility depends on the track: Campus Hiring is open to final-year students, Summer Internship to penultimate-year students, and Winter Internship to second and third-year students. Visit the Internship Program page for full details.

Some roles, particularly within the Digital & Technology practice, offer remote or hybrid arrangements. The Location field on each job listing will indicate whether a role is remote-eligible, hybrid, or based in a specific office.

Our process has five stages: application review, a recruiter screening call, a case and skills interview, a partner interview, and finally offer and onboarding. Most candidates complete the full process within four to six weeks.

We accept resumes in PDF, DOC, or DOCX format, up to 5 MB. A clear, single-column layout with quantified achievements is easiest for our reviewers to evaluate quickly.

After submitting, you'll receive a confirmation with a unique Application ID by email. Our recruitment team will update you by email at each stage; you can also reach out directly to careers@ambideradvisors.com with your Application ID.
