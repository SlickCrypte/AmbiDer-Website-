Recognition

AmbiDer Advisors & Management Consultants LLP proudly recognizes Akshay Rakshit as The Trailblazer (TRB) of the Week on The AmbiDer Brilliance Board (TABB) for his exceptional leadership in Human Resources, talent strategy, and organizational transformation. His inspiring journey reflects resilience, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Professional Journey

With over 12 years of HR leadership experience, Akshay Rakshit has built an impressive career across leading automotive organizations including Honda Cars India Ltd., Maruti Suzuki India Limited, BMW Group, and currently Hyundai Motor Company India. Starting from shop-floor industrial relations and negotiations, he steadily progressed into HR Business Partnering, Talent Acquisition, HR Transformation, Employer Branding, Leadership Hiring, Change Management, and Early Careers strategy.

Leadership Philosophy

A Jesuit-educated national-level athlete in swimming and basketball, Akshay transformed the lessons of sports into a powerful leadership philosophy centered on resilience, teamwork, discipline, and perseverance. Beyond the workplace, he actively mentors young professionals and frequently speaks at leading business schools, inspiring future leaders through his practical and simplified approach to solving complex business challenges.
